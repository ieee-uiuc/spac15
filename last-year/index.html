Workshops and Talks from SPAC 2013.

The Qualcomm Workshop

Last year, Jamie gave a workshop on making the transition from student life to entering the workforce can be tough as you encounter many. Attendees learned how to cope and adapt to new challenges like relocation, shift in accountability, adjusting to a new environment, realizing your new “social status”, learning outside of school, translating day 1, and understanding how to interpret, gather, and deal with feedback.

The Startup Experience

David Ahmari gave a great talk about the startup experience. He explained the various challenges faced in a startup and ways to solve them. Ahmari has authored over 40 patents, journal articles, and conference presentations and is a leading semiconductor device and materials expert.

What's Ahead: Overview of Engineering Careers

Marlin P. Ristenbatt gave a talk focusing on the attributes of a public career and private employment sectors, the different career stages, things that employers look for and career planning tips. Ristenbatt obtained a Ph.D in Electrical Engineering in 1960. He has worked on both military and civilian communication projects as Project Leader at the Communications and Signal Processing Laboratory of the University of Michigan.
